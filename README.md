# 🌊 Dive Circle

## Your Personal Diving Companion

Dive Circle is an offline-first Progressive Web App built by divers, for divers.

Our mission is simple:

> **Dive Circle is your personal diving companion. Community is an extension of your experience, not the purpose of the app.**

Whether you're preparing for your first dive or logging your thousandth, Dive Circle is designed to help you remember, plan, learn, and relive every dive.

---

# Why Dive Circle?

Most dive apps focus on logging dives or creating a social network.

Dive Circle starts somewhere different.

The diver comes first.

Every feature should make diving easier, safer, and more enjoyable.

Community features exist to enhance the individual diver's experience—not replace it.
